Get Involved

Our team is a friendly community of high school and college students from all around the U.S. and beyond, united by a passion of technology and helping students. You'll do rewarding work, build meaningful connections, grow skills, and uplift others. Time commitment is just a few hours a week, and is very flexible.

Join us by filling out one of our short applications and we'll get back to you shortly. Know someone who would be a perfect fit? Please share this with them, too!
