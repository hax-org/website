HAX Mentee Interest Form

HAX is an up-and-coming, student-run nonprofit making computer science education more accessible through hackathons and workshops. Through one-on-one mentorship and detailed guides, we make the process of organizing a hackathon easy and simple. We’re working with over 15 hackathons in three countries and are proudly partnered with Girls Who Code, #BUILTBYGIRLS, TILE, and Crowdera.

Interested in starting a hackathon, workshop, or event? Fill out this interest form, and we’ll get back to you shortly.
